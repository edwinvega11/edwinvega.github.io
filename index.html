About Me

I am Edwin Vega. I am currently studying computer science and aspire to be a software engineer. My biggest passion in life is technology. Software development is my favorite aspect of such a broad field, but it is not the only thing I enjoy. I have done pretty much everything there is to do with computer tech from video editing, graphic design, creating music with digital art workstations, building my own PC or just laying back with a good video game. Coding classes were one of the very first ways I delved into the world of 1s and 0s. When I was in 5th grade, one of the software engineers at my dads company offered to give me coding classes in the afternoon, which I immediately bit. I took those classes for about six months, and learned the Ruby scripting language. This gave me a very strong foundation on how coding works at a very young age. Details like how a variable works or how to use conditional statements have stuck with me and found use to this day as I learn a variety of programming languages.
